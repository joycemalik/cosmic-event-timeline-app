Cosmic Timeline

The Big Bang
13.8 Billion Years Ago
The universe began as an extremely hot, dense point that rapidly expanded.
First Stars Form
13.6 Billion Years Ago
Gravity drew together hydrogen and helium, igniting the first celestial giants.
Milky Way Galaxy Forms
13 Billion Years Ago
Our home galaxy began to coalesce, forming its distinctive spiral structure.
Formation of the Solar System
4.6 Billion Years Ago
A spinning cloud of gas and dust collapsed to form the Sun and planets.
First Life on Earth
3.8 Billion Years Ago
Single-celled organisms emerged in Earth's oceans, marking the dawn of biology.
