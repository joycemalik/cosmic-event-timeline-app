The Big Bang

13.8 Billion Years Ago

The universe began from a hot, dense singularity, expanding rapidly to form space and time.

First Stars Form

13.6 Billion Years Ago

Gravity drew together primordial hydrogen and helium, igniting the first luminous stars.

Milky Way Galaxy Forms

13 Billion Years Ago

Our home galaxy, the Milky Way, began to coalesce, accumulating billions of stars.

Formation of the Solar System

4.6 Billion Years Ago

A swirling disc of gas and dust collapsed to form the Sun, planets, and other celestial bodies.

First Life on Earth

3.8 Billion Years Ago

Simple single-celled organisms emerged in Earth's oceans, marking the dawn of life.

Dinosaurs Roam Earth

230 Million Years Ago

The Mesozoic Era began, a period dominated by diverse reptilian life forms across the globe.

Human Civilization

12,000 Years Ago

The development of agriculture led to permanent settlements and the rise of complex societies.
